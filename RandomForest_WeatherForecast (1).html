Random Forest¶

In this excercise, we are going to predict the weather temeperature for the next day in seattle, using weather data of one year of the city of seattle.

Data overview:

temp_2 - max temp two days prior
temp_1 - max temp one day prior
average - historical average max temperature
actual - actual temperature in 2016
friend - your friends prediction

You can find the excercise here. Thanks to William Koerhsen, who posted it on Medium.
